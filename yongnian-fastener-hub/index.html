How Overseas Buyers Source Fasteners from Yongnian

The sourcing process follows a structured workflow where buyer specifications are reviewed manually before quotation.

  1. Buyer specification — Buyer prepares product type, standard or drawing reference, dimensions, material, finish, quantity, packing requirements, and any required documentation.
  2. RFQ review — The sourcing partner reviews specifications, confirms technical feasibility, and identifies suitable supplier options within the Yongnian manufacturing network.
  3. Supplier matching — Supplier capabilities are matched against buyer requirements for product type, standard, material, finish, quantity, and lead time expectations.
  4. Sample or specification confirmation — Buyer specifications, drawings, or samples are confirmed before production. Product images and general descriptions are not final specifications.
  5. Production coordination — Production is coordinated with confirmed specifications, including material sourcing, manufacturing, heat treatment, surface finishing, and in-process quality checks.
  6. Packing and documentation — Packing quantities, carton and pallet specifications, labels, and inspection documents are prepared according to buyer requirements.
  7. Inspection and shipment — Final inspection is conducted per agreed requirements before shipment to the destination port or delivery point.
