They are not automatically interchangeable with standard flat washers.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>What is the difference between ASTM F844 and ASTM F436 washers?l/summary>lp>ASTM F844 covers general-purpose plain washers made from low-carbon steel. ASTM F844 washers must not be substituted for ASTM F436 hardened washers in structural assemblies.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>When are hardened washers required?l/summary>lp>Hardened washers are required when the buyer's project specification, applicable standard or structural bolting standard calls for them. Buyers should confirm the washer type, hardness, standard and project requirements.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>Can DIN 125 and ISO 7089 washers be treated as automatically identical?l/summary>lp>DIN 125 and ISO 7089 are related but may have differences in dimensions, tolerances, material requirements and product grade. Buyers should confirm the applicable standard, version, dimensions and requirements. Legacy DIN and current ISO references are not automatically identical in all details.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>Why must washer outside diameter and thickness be confirmed?l/summary>lp>Outside diameter and thickness affect the bearing area, load distribution and fit within the assembly. The same nominal bolt size can correspond to different outside diameters and thicknesses depending on the washer series and standard. These dimensions must be confirmed per the applicable standard or drawing.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>Which materials and finishes are suitable for outdoor use?l/summary>lp>Material and finish selection depends on the specific environmental conditions, including moisture, temperature, chemical exposure and design life. Stainless steel, hot-dip galvanized and zinc flake coatings are commonly considered for outdoor applications. Buyers should confirm the specific material, finish, thickness and performance requirements.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>Can hot-dip galvanized washers be matched with zinc-plated bolts?l/summary>lp>Coating compatibility should be reviewed. A washer that is too soft may deform under load. A washer that is incompatible with the bolt grade or application may not perform as intended. Hardness must be confirmed per the applicable standard and project requirements.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>Can different washer sizes be included in one mixed order?l/summary>lp>Yes.
