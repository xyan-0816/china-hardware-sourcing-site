Coating restrictions, matching nut grades and washer requirements also differ by grade.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>Can inch and metric structural bolts be substituted?l/summary>lp>No. Inch series (UNC thread, nominal inch diameters) and metric series (coarse metric thread, nominal millimetre diameters) are different systems and cannot be substituted without changing the project design.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>Why must matching nut grades be confirmed?l/summary>lp>Structural bolting assemblies require compatible nut grades, thread fit and coating. A nut that appears similar in size may not have the correct grade, strength or thread compatibility for the specified bolt grade and coating.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>Why are ASTM F436 hardened washers specified for structural assemblies?l/summary>lp>ASTM F436 hardened steel washers are designed to provide a proper bearing surface for structural bolt assemblies. Ordinary commercial washers may not have the hardness, flatness or hole size specified for structural connections.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>What is a direct tension indicator?l/summary>lp>A direct tension indicator (DTI) is a product used with structural bolting assemblies to indicate bolt tension during installation. It is not a plain washer and should be specified to ASTM F959/F959M where required by the project.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>Can all structural bolt grades be hot-dip galvanized?l/summary>lp>Coating suitability depends on the exact grade, type, dimensions, matching components and applicable project specification. It must be confirmed during manual RFQ review. Not all grades or types permit hot-dip galvanizing.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>Why must coating and thread fit be reviewed together?l/summary>lp>Coating thickness affects thread fit. Nuts may require oversize tapping to accommodate coating, which must be specified. Thread fit, nut grade and coating must be confirmed as a compatible system.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>Can bolts, nuts and washers be supplied as one assembly?l/summary>lp>Yes. Complete structural bolting assemblies can be reviewed manually when bolt grade, nut grade, washer type, coating, quantities and lot traceability requirements are specified.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>What inspection documents can be discussed before production?l/summary>lp>Depending on the item and buyer requirements, documents may include dimensional reports, material test certificates, coating thickness reports, hardness reports, rotational capacity test results where applicable, and pre-shipment photos.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>Can heavy hex bolts be included in a mixed fastener order?l/summary>lp>Yes. Final lead time is confirmed after RFQ review.l/p>l/details>l ldetails>lsummary>Can you review an RFQ that uses inch dimensions and convert it to metric references?l/summary>lp>Yes.
