FAQ

What should buyers confirm for Self-Drilling Screws?

Buyers should confirm drill point, head type, drive type, material, finish, and substrate thickness, plus quantity, packing, and required documents before RFQ review.

Are images enough for quotation?

No. Images are representative references only. Drawings, standards, samples, or purchase lists should define final requirements.

What documents may be requested?

Depending on the item, buyers may request dimensional checks, material documents, coating reports, packing lists, and pre-shipment photos.

Can this item be part of a mixed fastener order?

Yes, if SKU data, quantity, packing, carton weight, and finish requirements are clear.
