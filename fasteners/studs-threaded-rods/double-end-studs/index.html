Buyers should confirm the product type per their drawing or standard.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>What is the difference between a double-end stud and an anchor rod?l/summary>lp>A double-end stud is a general-purpose stud with threaded ends for nut or tapped-hole engagement. They serve different applications and are not interchangeable.l/p>l/details>ldetails>lsummary>What is the difference between a double-end stud and a headed bolt?l/summary>lp>A double-end stud has no head; it has threaded ends at both sides.
