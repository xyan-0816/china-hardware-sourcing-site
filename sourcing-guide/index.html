Sourcing Guide FAQ

Use these short answers before selecting the detailed page you need.

How should buyers use this sourcing guide?

Start with order planning, then verify supplier and QC documents, then confirm standards and finishes before RFQ follow-up.

Does the guide replace buyer engineering review?

No. Final requirements should be confirmed by the buyer's engineering, quality, purchasing, or project team.

Can buyers send mixed fastener lists?

Yes. Mixed lists can be reviewed manually when product details, quantities, packing requirements, and documents are provided.

Are public prices shown in the guide?

No. Requirements vary by specification, finish, quantity, packaging, and documents, so RFQs are reviewed manually.

What documents are useful before supplier approval?

ISO 9001 where relevant, business license, MTC/MTR samples, inspection report examples, and export documents are useful.

What should buyers confirm before placing production?

Confirm product standard, size, grade, material, finish, packing, inspection documents, and shipment constraints.
